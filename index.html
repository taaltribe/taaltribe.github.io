Taal Tribe

Kathak & Semi-Classical Dance Collective – Saarbrücken, Germany

About Us

Founded by Ivaxi Sheth in 2023, Taal Tribe celebrates the rhythm of Kathak and semi-classical dance forms. What started as a passion project evolved into a dynamic group of artists redefining South Asian storytelling through movement in Germany and beyond.

Our Story

Ivaxi, trained in Lucknow gharana, envisioned a space where dance could thrive not just as tradition but as lived, evolving expression. Today, Taal Tribe performs across stages and streets, inviting audiences into a tapestry of rhythm, expression, and heritage.

About Kathak

Kathak is one of the eight classical dance forms of India, rooted in storytelling through rhythmic footwork, spins, and expressive gestures. It bridges devotional expression and technical brilliance, evolving over centuries through temple performances and royal courts.
