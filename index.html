Taal Tribe

Kathak & Semi-Classical Dance Collective – Saarbrücken, Germany

About Us

Founded by Anaya Sharma in 2023, Taal Tribe celebrates the rhythm of Kathak and semi-classical dance forms. What started as a passion project evolved into a dynamic group of artists redefining South Asian storytelling through movement in Germany and beyond.

Our Story

Anaya, trained in Lucknow gharana, envisioned a space where dance could thrive not just as tradition but as lived, evolving expression. Today, Taal Tribe performs across stages and streets, inviting audiences into a tapestry of rhythm, expression, and heritage.
