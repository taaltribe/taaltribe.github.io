Welcome to Taal Tribe

A South Asian dance group rooted in tradition and rhythm. Taal Tribe brings Kathak and semi-classical forms to new spaces in Saarbrücken and beyond.

About Us

Founder: Anaya Sharma

Anaya founded Taal Tribe in 2023 to build a bridge between heritage and expression through classical Indian dance. Originally trained in Lucknow gharana Kathak, she envisioned a collective that celebrated tradition while embracing modern narratives. The group now performs across Germany, creating dynamic experiences through rhythm, storytelling, and movement.
